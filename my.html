About Company


RPK Tech is a full service recruitment firm that specializes in providing Engineering and Technical recruiting services, throughout the US, in the Aerospace / Defense, Energy, Environmental and Architecture industries


Our team of Engineering & Technology recruiting specialists can provide personalized services, to fit the changing needs of both our job seeking candidates, and our corporate clients. With many years of specialization in both the contingency search recruiting industry, and corporate talent management, STS's staff benefits from a thorough understanding of the disciplines and positions in the engineering field, and the specific needs of all parties involved in the recruiting life cycle.
